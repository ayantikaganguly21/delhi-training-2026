Overview
This repository contains all the hands-on projects completed during the Delhi Industrial Training Program organized by Adamas University from 16th February 2026 to 26th February 2026.
The training focused on practical exposure to industry-relevant technologies, real-world problem solving, AI tools, and system development. Each project included implementation, testing, and documentation.
Objectives of the Training
To gain real-world industrial exposure
To develop practical implementation skills
To understand AI and emerging technologies
To enhance problem-solving and teamwork abilities
To build deployable mini-projects
